Major Gifts

A gift of $5,000 or more makes an incredible impact in the communities we serve. Since One Acre Fund’s model requires just $25 of donor funding to deliver our complete service bundle to one farmer – your contribution is enough to bring critical farm inputs and training to hundreds of farm families for an entire year.

If you are interested in learning more about how your major gift will fuel our proven, scalable solution to end extreme hunger alongside East Africa’s smallholder farmers, please feel free to contact us.

tinnos farm trip chapters are networks of people who passionately have ideas of changing african agriculture into modern farming and bringing honour and dignity to farmers. They are committed collaborators keen on activating global change with their local communities.

tinnos farmtrip members support our work to support farmersin rural areas where we are tripping and trekking towards and we contribute socially to the wellbeing of smallholders.
